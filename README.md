# Word To Latex: The Researcher's Guide to LaTeX Formatting

**Word-to-LaTeX Conversion, Pdf to Latex conversion. Journal Templates, Compilation Errors, and Thesis Formatting**

A free, practical guide for PhD students, postdocs, and academics who need to work with LaTeX for journal submissions and thesis formatting.

📄 **[Download the free PDF guide](./latex-formatting-guide.pdf)**

---

## What's Inside

**Chapter 1 – Introduction**
Who the guide is for and what problems it solves.

**Chapter 2 – Converting Word Documents to LaTeX**
Two-stage workflow using Pandoc: automated conversion + manual adaptation to journal templates. Includes common output issues and fixes.

**Chapter 3 – Converting PDF to LaTeX**
When and how to convert PDFs, including Mathpix, Meta's Nougat, and the hybrid manual approach.

**Chapter 4 – The 10 Most Common LaTeX Compilation Errors**
Exact error messages, causes, and fixes for every error researchers encounter — from `Undefined control sequence` to corrupted auxiliary files.

**Chapter 5 – Formatting for Major Journal Templates**
Template-specific guidance for IEEE (IEEEtran), Elsevier (elsarticle), Springer (svjour3 / LNCS), ACM (acmart), and MDPI — including the exact mistakes most researchers make.

**Chapter 6 – PhD Thesis Formatting in LaTeX**
Structure, essential packages, margin/spacing requirements, and converting a Word thesis to LaTeX.

**Chapter 7 – Bibliography Management That Actually Works**
BibTeX vs. BibLaTeX, the correct 4-command compilation sequence, and the most common .bib mistakes.

**Chapter 8 – Submitting to Journal Systems Without Losing Your Mind**
Elsevier Editorial Manager, ScholarOne, and arXiv — why submissions fail and how to fix them. Includes a universal pre-submission checklist.

**Chapter 9 – Quick Reference**
Essential packages, compilation commands, and emergency fixes on one page.

---

## Who This Is For

- PhD students converting a Word thesis to LaTeX
- Researchers reformatting a paper for a specific journal template
- Anyone stuck on a LaTeX compilation error with a submission deadline tomorrow
- Authors submitting to Editorial Manager, ScholarOne, or arXiv

This guide assumes you know what LaTeX is and have compiled at least one document before. It is **not** a beginner LaTeX tutorial — it covers the practical problems that come after you know the basics.
